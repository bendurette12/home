POMB

  • There is no ultimate meaning/purpose in life (at least that we can be aware of)

  • Yet, I choose to live any way, and if I'm going to live, I may as well do it well

  • Change is the only constant. It should be embraced, rather than feared.

  • Growth comes through discomfort

  • I need goals and to be challenged, both big and small

  • Deep, meaningful relationships are possibly the most important and life giving parts of my life

  • A healthy social life (friends, community, activities, etc...) is important as well

  • Consistent exercise and meditation are mandatory

  • Always keep an open mind, but critique everything before accepting anything

  • Discipline and order are more fulfilling than disorder, but also allow room for laziness and spontaneity

  • Learning is possibly the second most important and life giving part of my life

  • Always read

  • There is rarely a reason to actually be mad at someone. Rather, understand their opinions/actions by looking at life from their perspective (shoutout to Marcus Aurelius)

  • Accept your faults and be compassionate to yourself while constantly working to improve

  • Life is limited, choose what to prioritize without much regret

  • You think a lot, bro. Give yourself space to process and think, but know when to cut yourself off before getting too bogged down

  • Life can be lonely, surround yourself with people who love you, challenge you, and let you feel (mostly) understood
